Ronaldo

Cristiano Ronaldo is a Portuguese professional footballer widely regarded as one of the greatest players of all time. Born on February 5, 1985, in Funchal, Madeira, he began his career with Sporting Lisbon before joining Manchester United in 2003, where he won multiple Premier League titles and the UEFA Champions League. In 2009, he transferred to Real Madrid for a then-record fee, becoming the club's all-time leading scorer and winning four more Champions League titles. Ronaldo is known for his incredible goal-scoring ability, athleticism, and dedication to training, which have contributed to his long-lasting success. Throughout his career, he has won numerous individual awards, including five Ballon d'Or trophies, and continues to inspire millions of fans worldwide.
